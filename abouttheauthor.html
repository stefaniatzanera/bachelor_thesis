   Hello! My name is Stefania Tzanera, I am 23 years old and I am half Greek and half Bulgarian. I live in Nikaia, Piraeus, and during the past four years, I study in the city of Tripolis. I am a student in the Department of Informatics and Telecommunications at the University of Peloponnese, and this web page is part of my thesis. I would love to get involved with web applications and design, thus, I chose Mr. Tselikas as my supervisor, as he is quite familiar with the field and can guide me properly.

   I was asked to implement a virtual tour guide of a place, so I chose my house for this purpose. The goal of this work is to provide the user with a tool, such that he/she is able to be guided around an area and interact with objects in various ways, thus enhancing his overall virtual tour guide experience.
